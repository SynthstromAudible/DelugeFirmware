This page hosts a locally integrated version of the Deluge Companion shortcuts explorer, updated for <Badge text="c1.3" variant="tip" />

Full credit to [Florian Heft](https://fheft.github.io/deluge-companion/) and [Matthew](https://handeyeco.github.io/deluge-shortcuts) for their original implementations.

<details>
  <summary>Caution</summary>
  <Aside type="caution">
    Note: This page is still a work in progress. Shortcuts may be incorrect
    and/or missing. Please send us feedback on GitHub, and/or Discord to improve
    this tool!
  </Aside>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Tips</summary>
  <Aside type="tip">
    - Use the search and filters panel to quickly find shortcuts. - Click on a
    shortcut to show the controls on the Deluge interface.
  </Aside>
</details>
